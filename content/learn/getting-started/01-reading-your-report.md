Your dashboard pulls together everything happening across your website, Google
Business Profile, and search rankings into one place. Here's how to read it.

## The KPI cards

At the top of the dashboard you'll see a row of cards. Each shows a **current
number** and a **change** compared to the previous period:

- **Sessions** — how many visits your website received.
- **Leads** — form submissions and calls captured from your site.
- **Local rank** — your average position in the Google map pack for the
  keywords we track.

A green change is an improvement; a red change means the number went down versus
the prior period.

## Choosing a date range

Use the date selector to switch between the last 7, 30, or 90 days. Every chart
and card updates to match, and the "change" values compare against the period
immediately before the one you picked.

## What to focus on

You don't need to watch every number every day. Month over month, the trend that
matters most is **leads** — that's the business outcome. Rankings and traffic are
the leading indicators that drive it.

If anything looks off, reply to your weekly digest email and we'll take a look.
